Our Story & Commitment

At ProGear, we've been powering athletes and enthusiasts with pro-grade fitness equipment since 2015. From industrial-strength frames to cutting-edge smart tracking, every product is designed, tested, and perfected in our North Macedonia facility.

We believe in performance, reliability, and sustainability. That's why we use eco-friendly materials, back every sale with our 5-year frame warranty, and partner with local gyms to give back to the community.

Our Core Values

Uncompromising Quality

Every product undergoes 50+ point inspection before shipping.

Continuous Innovation

We invest 15% of revenue back into R&D each year.

Sustainability First

Eco-friendly materials and 100% recyclable packaging.

Community Focus

Supporting local gyms and sharing free workout plans.
